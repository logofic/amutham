About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

Opening our restaurants has been a challenging, new and exciting experience for us. We started the restaurant to provide budget friendly, high class experience also serving tasty food for the people. The response surprised even us, it was overwhelming even within a very short time.

The vision we set out for ourselves when we started is value for money, where our customers and their experience with our food and service are of paramount importance to us. When we say value, we mean quality. And to maintain nutritious content, taste and consistency, we source the best ingredients, have trained home grown chefs and have centralized kitchens to prepare our food. We have centralized kitchens preparing our core ingredients and dishes in order to maintain quality and consistency.
